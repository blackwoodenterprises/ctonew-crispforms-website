Frequently Asked Questions

Is CrispForms really free?

Yes! We offer unlimited forms and unlimited submissions completely free. All core features are included at no cost.

How long does support take to respond?

We typically respond to support inquiries within 24 hours on business days. Urgent issues are prioritized.

Do you offer phone support?

Currently, we provide email support and comprehensive documentation. Phone support may be available for enterprise customers.

Can I request a new feature?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. Send us your feature requests and we'll consider them for our roadmap.

How do I report a bug?

Email us at support@crispforms.com with details about the bug, including steps to reproduce it and any error messages.

Do you have a status page?

Yes, you can check our service status and uptime at status.crispforms.com for real-time updates.
