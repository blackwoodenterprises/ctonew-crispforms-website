Acceptable Use Policy

Last Updated: October 17, 2024

1. Introduction

This Acceptable Use Policy ("Policy") describes prohibited uses of CrispForms' services. By using our Service, you agree to comply with this Policy. Violation of this Policy may result in suspension or termination of your account.

2. Prohibited Activities

2.1 Illegal Activities

You may not use CrispForms to:

  • Violate any local, state, national, or international law
  • Engage in fraud, scams, or deceptive practices
  • Facilitate illegal transactions or activities
  • Distribute illegal content or materials
  • Infringe on intellectual property rights
  • Violate data protection or privacy laws

2.2 Harmful Content

You may not create forms or collect data that:

  • Promote violence, terrorism, or hate speech
  • Harass, threaten, or intimidate individuals
  • Contain explicit or adult content without proper age verification
  • Distribute malware, viruses, or harmful code
  • Exploit or harm minors in any way
  • Promote self-harm or dangerous activities

2.3 Spam and Phishing

You may not use CrispForms to:

  • Send unsolicited bulk messages or spam
  • Conduct phishing attacks or identity theft
  • Impersonate others or misrepresent your identity
  • Create fake or misleading forms
  • Collect personal information through deception

2.4 Abusive Behavior

You may not:

  • Abuse, threaten, or harass CrispForms staff or other users
  • Submit false reports or abuse our support system
  • Attempt to gain unauthorized access to accounts or systems
  • Interfere with other users' ability to use the Service
  • Attempt to bypass security measures or restrictions

2.5 System Abuse

You may not:

  • Overload or attempt to disable our servers or infrastructure
  • Launch denial-of-service (DoS) or distributed denial-of-service (DDoS) attacks
  • Use automated tools to scrape or harvest data without permission
  • Reverse engineer, decompile, or attempt to extract source code
  • Probe, scan, or test vulnerabilities in our systems
  • Circumvent rate limits or usage restrictions

2.6 Competitive Use

You may not:

  • Use the Service to build a competing product or service
  • Benchmark the Service for competitive purposes without consent
  • Copy or replicate features, design, or functionality
  • Collect data about the Service for competitive advantage

3. Data Collection Restrictions

3.1 Consent and Transparency

When collecting data through forms, you must:

  • Obtain proper consent from respondents
  • Clearly explain what data you're collecting and why
  • Provide a privacy policy if collecting personal information
  • Honor respondents' data rights (access, deletion, etc.)
  • Comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy laws

3.2 Prohibited Data Collection

You may not collect:

  • Sensitive personal information without explicit consent and proper security measures
  • Information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • Financial information without proper security and compliance
  • Health information without HIPAA compliance (where applicable)
  • Authentication credentials or passwords

4. Fair Use Policy

While we offer unlimited forms and submissions on the free plan, you must use resources reasonably:

  • Do not abuse system resources or create excessive load
  • Do not use the Service in a way that negatively impacts other users
  • We reserve the right to throttle or limit usage that exceeds reasonable thresholds
  • Sustained excessive usage may result in account review

5. Content Moderation

CrispForms reserves the right to:

  • Monitor content for compliance with this Policy
  • Remove or disable access to content that violates this Policy
  • Report illegal content to law enforcement authorities
  • Cooperate with legal investigations

6. Reporting Violations

If you become aware of any violations of this Policy, please report them to:

  • Email: abuse@crispforms.com
  • Contact Form: Contact Us

Please include relevant details such as form URLs, account information, and description of the violation.

7. Consequences of Violations

Violations of this Policy may result in:

  • Warning: First-time or minor violations may result in a warning
  • Temporary Suspension: Repeated or moderate violations may result in temporary account suspension
  • Permanent Termination: Serious or repeated violations will result in permanent account termination
  • Legal Action: We may pursue legal action for illegal activities
  • Law Enforcement: We may report criminal activities to appropriate authorities

8. Appeals

If you believe your account was suspended or terminated in error:

  • Contact us at appeals@crispforms.com within 30 days
  • Provide detailed explanation and any supporting evidence
  • We will review your appeal and respond within 10 business days
  • Our decision on appeals is final

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Acceptable Use Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

10. Contact Information

For questions about this Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: legal@crispforms.com
  • Contact Form: Contact Us
